Projects

Rights-a-quest


Rights-a-quest is a gamified visual novel focused towards children. It aims to raise legal awareness among children and guide them through real life scenarios. It has multiple pathways that the player can complete to gain reward points. Notably, Cyberbullying, Harrassment at school and Road Safety. It has support for one additional regional language-Telugu.


The game was developed using a Visual Novel Game Engine -- Ren'Py, A Chat Support screen was added by deploying an LLM on Microsoft Azure. The applicaiton was not launched but the preview was presented at the National Level Hackathon at CBIT, Hyderabad, India.

Disease Prediction System


I developed a sophisticated SVM (Support Vector Machine)-based classification system designed to predict diseases such as diabetes, heart disease, and pneumonia. This system leverages machine learning to provide accurate and reliable predictions. For image classification, particularly for diagnosing pneumonia, I utilized advanced deep learning algorithms, specifically Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs). These CNNs were trained on a large dataset of chest X-rays, enabling the system to accurately identify and classify pneumonia from these images. By combining SVM for structured data classification with CNNs for image analysis, the disease prediction system offers a comprehensive approach to medical diagnostics.
